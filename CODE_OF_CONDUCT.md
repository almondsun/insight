# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Contributors must communicate respectfully, give actionable technical feedback, respect privacy, and avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, or publishing another person's private information.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository owner. Reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions and temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its community.

This policy applies in project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
